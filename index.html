About Me

I like to make things, and do basketball, and like tech, here's what makes me, me:

  • Game Creator Extraordinaire: I love making my own video games and software! It's like building my own world where I decide the rules. I use computers to bring my ideas to life, and it's so cool to play something I made.
  • Computer Whiz: I'm crazy about computers! I like learning how they work and using them for fun projects. They're like magic tools for creating and exploring.
  • Robot Champ: I was part of a super awesome team called Robot Subs in First LEGO League (FLL). We built and programmed robots, and guess what? We made it all the way to regionals and won the Rising All-Star award, celebrating a team the judges expect great things from in the future. We also won the Engineering Excellence Award for an efficiently designed robot and an innovative project solution!
  • Basketball Star: When I'm not at the computer, you'll find me on the basketball court. I love dribbling, shooting hoops, and playing with my friends—it's so much fun to stay active!
  • Master Builder: I love making things, whether it's a cool LEGO creation, a craft, or something totally new. I'm always coming up with ideas and figuring out how to build them.
  • Slick Ninja: I go to a ninja class where I learned how to do a front flip and other cool moves. My coach is nice, and I go every Thursday—it's fun!

If you want to talk about games, robots, basketball, or being a ninja, I'm your guy!
